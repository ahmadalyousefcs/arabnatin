Mansaf

Mansaf porridge of lamb, yogurt and local wheat bread or rice. It is the most famous meal in Jordan, and it extends to the Badia al-Sham region located between Iraq and Jordan, southern Syria and northern Saudi Arabia. Jordanians are considered the most famous for its preparation, as it is the national dish in Jordan, and it is cooked at weddings, funerals and various occasions.
